about us

what makes our coffee special?

From bean to cup we take painstaking care over our coffee, ensuring that you cannot get an experience like it. It is not just for wakening up in the morning!It is our steady commitment to sustainability, quality and what came to be called Rollover that makes us unique.

First of all, we care about where it comes from. To maintain high quality, Rollover builds up with farmers all around the globe good relationships between supplier and buyer. This direct link not only means people in source countries receive a fair price for their work (thus improving lives directly) but also offers us access to some of the best most interesting beans.
